Our Business

Keylytics operates an online content platform that focuses on keyword research and content creation. By analyzing search volumes and trends, we identify which topics have the most interest and produce articles that match user demand.

Use of Google Ads API

We plan to use the Google Ads API to analyze keyword metrics such as search volume and competition. This data will be used internally by our team to support content strategy and ensure our articles remain relevant and useful.

Tool Design

Our keyword analysis dashboard allows input of keywords and displays estimated metrics. While current data is mock-up, future versions will integrate actual API data and generate reports to guide decision-making.
