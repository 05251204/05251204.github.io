Our Business

Keylytics operates an online content platform focused on delivering articles and insights based on keyword popularity. We research keywords and their related subkeywords to identify which topics have high search interest, and create content that provides useful and relevant information for our audience.

Use of Google Ads API

We plan to use the Google Ads API to gather keyword metrics such as search volumes and trends. This information will be used internally by our team to guide content creation and ensure our articles address topics that users are most interested in.

Tool Design

Our tool will provide a dashboard that allows our team to input keywords and instantly view estimated search volumes. In the future, we plan to expand the tool to include reporting and trend analysis. All data will be used internally to support our content strategy.
